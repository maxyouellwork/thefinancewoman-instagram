Myth 1
"My accountant handles all my financial planning"
Reality
Accountants handle tax. Pensions, protection, and mortgage planning are a completely different discipline — and they often get completely missed.
Myth 2
"I don't earn enough for a pension to be worth it"
Reality
Tax relief means HMRC effectively tops up what you put in. Even £50/month now compounds significantly. The sooner you start, the less you need to contribute overall.
Myth 3
"Life insurance is only for people with children"
Reality
It covers mortgages, protects partners, and settles business debts. You don't need children for it to matter — and it's usually cheapest when you're young and healthy.
Myth 4
"I'll sort my finances out when things settle down"
Reality
Things rarely settle. And every month you wait is a month your pension isn't growing, a month you're unprotected, a month of opportunity cost. The best time to start is now.
